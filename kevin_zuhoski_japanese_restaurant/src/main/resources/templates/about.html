History of Shibukawa

Our restaurant, founded in 2020, named after the enchanting and idyllic city in which it resides. Shibukawa Restaurant, whose name in Japanese contains the word "kawa" meaning river, overlooks the majestic and heavenly Tonegawa river, and provides diners with a breathtaking panoramic view of the river as they dine. Our dishes are inspired by and infused with local fruits and vegetables like daikon and persimmon, that are freshly picked and delivered daily by Shibukawa's own farmers. Along with our exquisite cuisine our restaurant provides an ambience that mirrors the simple and subtle beauty of the surrounding mountains, forests, rice fields, and rivers. Tourists can spend their day hiking in the picturesque mountains, or indulging in the nearby soothing and relaxing hot springs, before concluding their evening with an otherwordly dining experience they will never forget.
